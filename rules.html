General Rules for SAC

  1. Students are allowed to use the SAC only during the following hours:
    • Working (Instruction) Days: 5:30 am to 8:30 am and 4:00 pm to 11:00 pm.
    • Holidays (No Instruction) Days: 5:30 am to 10:00 pm.
    • The President, Vice President, and The General Secretaries of the SGC may request to use the SAC beyond the above timing for special occasions.
  2. Students must carry their Institutional identity card to enter into SAC. If the Institutional identity cards are unavailable (say for first-year students), they may use any government identity card. Students must submit their identity cards to security personnel before entering the SAC and collect their respective identity cards before leaving the SAC. Only hard-copy identity cards are allowed.
  3. Students entering the SAC must register their entry. They need to mention which rooms they want to use.
  4. Students should ensure proper hygiene and contribute to the cleanliness of the SAC. Students are not permitted to enter the SAC with dirty/muddy shoes. Students must not litter the SAC. No eateries, except water and sports drinks, are allowed in the SAC.
  5. Students should put equipment back in their respective dedicated positions.
  6. Students damaging any equipment/infrastructure of the SAC are liable to fines and will be dealt with as per student code of conduct.
  7. Each Board may impose additional requirements for entering the associated rooms and accessing the associated equipment.
  8. The Security Personnel reserves the right to deny entry to students not following the above rule. Non-compliance may result in permanent debarment from using the SAC.
  9. The SGC Members reserve the right to conduct periodic inspections of the equipment and facilities to ensure proper maintenance and usage. Club Coordinators will be notified in advance of any scheduled inspections.
  10. Any proposed modifications or decorations must be approved by the associated Faculty Coordinators before implementation.
  11. Any concerns or issues regarding the rooms or their activities should be reported promptly to the associated General Secretaries.

Rules for Using the Badminton Court under Sports Board

  1. Students are only allowed to enter the badminton court if they wear the proper badminton shoes (non-marking shoes).
  2. Students willing to use this room must carry their shoes separately (maybe in a bag) and show them to security at the entry.

Rules for Using the Table Tennis Court under Sports Board

  1. Students are only allowed to enter the table tennis court if they wear the proper non-marking shoes.
  2. Students willing to use this room must carry their shoes separately (maybe in a bag) and show them to security at the entry.

Rules for Using the Gym in Student Activity Center

  1. Timings: Working (Instruction) Days: 5:30 am to 8:30 am and 4:00 pm to 11:00 pm.
    Holidays (No Instruction) Days: 5:30 am to 10:00 pm.
  2. Membership Requirement: Only registered students, faculty, and staff members of IIIT Guwahati are allowed to use the gym facilities. Valid identification must be presented upon entry.
  3. Attire and Footwear: Proper gym attire, including athletic clothing and non-marking sports shoes, must be worn at all times. Barefoot training is prohibited for safety reasons.
  4. Equipment Usage: Users are expected to handle gym equipment with care and follow the instructions provided for each machine. Hogging or monopolizing equipment is not allowed, and users must respect time limits during peak hours if applicable.
  5. Safety and Hygiene: Users must maintain a clean and hygienic environment by wiping down equipment after use and returning weights and other accessories to their designated places. Personal hygiene, including the use of towels and deodorants, is essential.
  6. Health and Medical Conditions: Users with pre-existing medical conditions, injuries, or disabilities should refrain from using the gym facilities.
  7. Noise and Disturbance: Users should avoid creating excessive noise or disturbances that may disrupt others' workouts. Loud music, shouting, or any behavior deemed disruptive is not permitted.
  8. Personal Belongings: Users are responsible for securing their valuables.
  9. Etiquette and Respect: Users must adhere to gym etiquette, including respecting other individuals' space, refraining from inappropriate behavior or language.

These rules are designed to ensure the safety, comfort, and enjoyment of all users while using the gym facilities. Violation of any rules may result in disciplinary action, including temporary or permanent suspension of gym privileges.

Additional rules will follow as and when new facilities will become operational.
