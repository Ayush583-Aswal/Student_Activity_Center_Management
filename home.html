About

The inauguration of the Student Activity Centre (SAC) by the esteemed director, Professor Dr. Gautam Barua on 26th January 2024, marks a significant milestone in the institute's pursuit of excellence and commitment to fostering the holistic development of IIITG students. With its array of world-class facilities, including badminton courts, dedicated rooms for music, drama, dance, table tennis, yoga, and art, the SAC stands as a testament to the institution's dedication to providing students with opportunities for diverse and enriching experiences beyond the classroom.
