Technical Board

The Technical Board within the Student Activity Center (SAC) serves as a dynamic nucleus for students passionate about delving into and expanding their expertise across diverse technical realms. As a central authority on technical pursuits within the campus, this board orchestrates a plethora of activities, initiatives, and resources aimed at nurturing students' technical acumen and fostering innovation. From organizing workshops and hackathons to facilitating industry collaborations and research endeavors, the Technical Board provides an indispensable platform for students to delve into hands-on learning, collaborative problem-solving, and cutting-edge technological advancements. By transcending the boundaries of conventional classroom education, the Technical Board empowers students to explore emerging technologies, cultivate practical skills, and shape the future of innovation. Through its unwavering commitment to excellence and ingenuity, the Technical Board cultivates a vibrant ecosystem where students can thrive, innovate, and contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
